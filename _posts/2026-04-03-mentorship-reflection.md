In every student's life, there comes a mentor who changes everything. For me, that was **Dr. Bilal**.

During our first semester's Technical Exhibition, he guided our team through the complexities of data science and machine learning. His belief in our potential pushed us to build the **Kidney Disease Prediction System**, which was a huge success.

Mentors don't just teach you what's in the books—they teach you how to think.
