In a world full of high-level languages like Python and JavaScript, we shouldn't forget **C++**.

For engineers, **performance and control** are paramount. Whether it's building firmware for an IoT device or real-time trading engines, C++ provides direct access to memory and incredible speed.

As a Computer Engineering student, mastering C++ is not optional—it's a requirement for anyone who wants to understand how computers truly work!
